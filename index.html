Mission

We believe that catering to the students in the College in this innovative manner, can not only revolutionize the College Canteen System but also aid us in fostering the spirit of Women's Entrepreneurship.


To serve healthy, delicious, and tailored dishes from diverse cuisines to the College Community in a hygienic, digital and seamless manner.


Our one of a kind solution, to counter the not so talked about, yet notoriously popular problem among students is planned to be a game-changer in the College Pantry and Food service system. With the foresight vision and crafty convicted mission of our founders, we aim to revolutionize the pre-existing system of Food services in Colleges.
